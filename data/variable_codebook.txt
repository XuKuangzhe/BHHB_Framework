Total Variables: 25
Total Observations: 4,200 (Example size from snippet)
Missing Data: None (Rows with missing validity or tracking data were removed during preprocessing)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. EXPERIMENTAL METADATA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
participant         [Character]
    Unique identifier for each participant (e.g., "Subj1").
    Anonymized for privacy.

expName             [Character]
    Identifier for the experimental session/condition.
    - "S8" : Experiment 1 (8-second duration condition, or specific setting 1)
    - "S12": Experiment 2 (12-second duration condition, or specific setting 2)
    *Note: "inferEx..." in raw data corresponds to the PsychoPy experiment name.

picname             [Character]
    Filename of the stimulus image presented (e.g., "AgrM1.jpg").
    Contains embedded information about the impression category (e.g., "Agr" = Agreeableness) 
    and model characteristics.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. BEHAVIORAL RESPONSES (Explicit)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
key_resp_2.keys     [Numeric]
    Explicit Impression Rating.
    Range: 1 (Low) to 7 (High).
    The participant's subjective evaluation of the face on the target trait.

key_resp_confi.keys [Numeric]
    Confidence Rating.
    Range: 1 (Low Confidence) to 7 (High Confidence).
    Self-reported confidence in the impression rating provided above.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. PARTICIPANT DEMOGRAPHICS & TRAITS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
age                 [Numeric]
    Participant's age in years.

gender              [Character]
    Participant's gender (e.g., "Male", "Female").

Big Five Personality Traits (Self-Reported):
Scores derived from the short-form Big Five inventory.
    Ext             [Numeric]: Extraversion score.
    Agr             [Numeric]: Agreeableness score.
    Con             [Numeric]: Conscientiousness score.
    Neu             [Numeric]: Neuroticism score.
    Ope             [Numeric]: Openness to Experience score.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. DATA QUALITY METRICS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Valp                [Numeric]
    Validity Percentage / Data Quality Score.
    Range: 0.0 to 1.0.
    Represents the proportion of valid gaze samples captured by the eye tracker 
    during the trial duration. 
    *Trials with Valp < 0.6 are typically excluded from analysis.*

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. GAZE TRACKING METRICS (Implicit)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Weighted density scores calculated from Gaussian-smoothed fixation heatmaps 
for specific Areas of Interest (AOIs).
Values represent the relative attention intensity on each facial feature.

GazeGL              [Numeric]: Glabella (Area between eyebrows)
GazeFH              [Numeric]: Forehead
GazeEB              [Numeric]: Eyebrows (Left + Right)
GazeEY              [Numeric]: Eyes (Left + Right)
GazeNO              [Numeric]: Nose
GazeMO              [Numeric]: Mouth

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. MOUSE TRACKING METRICS (Implicit)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Weighted density scores calculated from Gaussian-smoothed mouse cursor heatmaps.
Analogous to Gaze metrics but reflecting manual exploration behavior.

MouseGL             [Numeric]: Glabella
MouseFH             [Numeric]: Forehead
MouseEB             [Numeric]: Eyebrows
MouseEY             [Numeric]: Eyes
MouseNO             [Numeric]: Nose
MouseMO             [Numeric]: Mouth
